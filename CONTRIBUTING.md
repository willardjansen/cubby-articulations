# Contributing

## Upload Your Maps

**[Click here to upload](https://github.com/willardjansen/cubby-articulations/discussions/new?category=share-your-maps)** - drag & drop your files, tell us what library it's for. Done.

---

## Rules

**OK to share:**
- Maps you made yourself
- Free/open-source maps (with credit)
- Official free releases from developers

**NOT OK:**
- Paid products (Art Conductor, etc.)
- Commercial map packs you bought
- Anything you don't have rights to share

We remove unauthorized content immediately.
